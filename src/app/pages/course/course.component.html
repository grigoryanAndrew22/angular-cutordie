Difficulty: 5

Duration: 2h 00m

Equipment:

This course is designed for hairstylists looking to push their creative limits. You'll learn advanced cutting and styling techniques to create sculptural, avant-garde hairstyles suitable for editorial work, runway shows, and competitions. We'll explore the history of avant-garde hair design and its influence on contemporary fashion. You'll also learn to draw inspiration from various art forms and translate abstract concepts into hair sculptures.

We'll cover a range of unconventional techniques including precision carving, 3D texturing, and asymmetrical design. You'll also learn how to incorporate non-hair materials and accessories into your creations. We'll delve into the properties of different hair types and how to manipulate them for sculptural effects. You'll experiment with innovative tools and products specifically designed for avant-garde styling, and learn to create your own custom tools when necessary.

By the end of the course, you'll have the skills to conceptualize and execute high-impact, artistic hairstyles. We'll also discuss how to market these skills for editorial and high-fashion clients. You'll learn about the business side of avant-garde hair design, including how to price your work, collaborate with photographers and fashion designers, and build a network in the fashion industry. We'll also cover techniques for documenting your creations and creating a standout portfolio that showcases your unique artistic vision.
